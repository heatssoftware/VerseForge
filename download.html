Windows SmartScreen & unsigned/unregistered apps

If Windows displays a SmartScreen warning that says "Windows protected your PC" or that the app is from an unknown publisher, this is normal for new or unsigned builds.

To run the installer safely:

  • Click "More info" on the SmartScreen dialog.
  • Choose "Run anyway".
